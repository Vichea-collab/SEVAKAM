Sevakam Subscription

Who pays
- Only providers pay for subscription plans.
- Finders use the app for free.

Payment
- Stripe is live.
- KHQR is planned later.

Plans

Basic
- Price: Free
- For: New providers
- Includes:
  - Basic profile
  - Direct chat
  - Standard search ranking
  - Up to 5 booking requests per month
  - 7-day new-provider boost

Professional
- Price: $10/month or $100/year
- For: Providers who want more visibility
- Includes:
  - Blue Professional badge
  - 2x search ranking boost
  - Up to 25 booking requests per month
  - Up to 15 portfolio photos
  - Profile analytics
  - 7-day free trial for new providers

Elite Business
- Price: $25/month or $250/year
- For: Top providers and teams
- Includes:
  - Gold Elite badge
  - 5x search ranking boost
  - Homepage exposure
  - Unlimited booking requests
  - Unlimited portfolio photos
  - Premium visibility

KYC and subscription
- KYC and subscription are separate.
- KYC approved = Verified badge.
- Subscription = plan badge like Professional or Elite.
- A provider can show both badges at the same time.

Current app behavior
- Finder side:
  - Shows provider plan badge on public provider cards and detail pages
  - Shows Verified badge separately when KYC is approved
- Provider side:
  - Provider manages subscription in the provider portal
  - Provider can see current plan, billing period, and renewal status
- Admin side:
  - Admin reviews subscriptions in the Subscriptions section
  - Admin reviews KYC in the Provider Verification section

Business rules
- Yearly billing gives discount
- Booking count resets each billing period
- Cancel takes effect at end of billing period
- Downgrade keeps current plan until the period ends

Important note
- Quality-gate text for paid plans is shown in the app UI
- It is not fully enforced in checkout yet
